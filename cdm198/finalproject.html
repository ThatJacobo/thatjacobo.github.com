This page contains info and suppiments for this paper

This video contains the clips used for the finals of my university classes, CDM 198 as well as CTS 040B. These were used to explain the interpolation within an animator's context. For the paper a total of 7 figures are shown, 6 of which are present in the above video compilation.

>Ex. 1 - DES 150 Motionography Example

This piece is the only one of the bunch NOT made specifically for this project. Within the paper this is Fig. 2 out of the 7 shown. Initially Ex. 3 was going to be taking this slot but after Ex.3 was finished, I decided it was too subtle to be used as an example within the paper. With my limited time leftover, I decided to include this piece as it was a fantastic example of shapes being moved and messed with using interpolation.
